Marco Prayer Galletti

Co-Founder & CTO at ReSmart

I'm the Co-Founder and CTO of ReSmart (formerly RecycleSmart), Australia's door-to-door recycling collection service. We help households and businesses recycle hard-to-recycle items—soft plastics, textiles, e-waste—that would otherwise end up in landfill.

With over 20 years in software development, I lead our engineering team building route optimisation, data intelligence, and platforms serving councils and communities across multiple Australian states.

Originally from Florence, Italy. Now based in Sydney.
